# Exercise Needs

## Dogs
Most adult dogs need 30 to 120 minutes of exercise per day depending on breed.
High-energy breeds like Border Collies, Australian Shepherds, and Huskies need
the upper end. Border Collies are extremely active and intelligent herding dogs;
they need vigorous daily walks, running, fetch, and agility to stay mentally
and physically healthy. Without enough exercise, Border Collies can develop
destructive or anxious behaviors. Senior dogs need shorter, gentler walks.

## Cats
Cats benefit from 10 to 15 minutes of active play, two or three times per day.
Wand toys, laser pointers (always finishing with a tangible toy to "catch"),
and food puzzles all work well.

## Warning signs of over-exercise
Excessive panting, lameness, reluctance to continue, or collapse are signs to
stop immediately. In hot weather, exercise early or late in the day and watch
for heatstroke symptoms.
