# Routine Health Care

## Vaccinations
Core vaccines for dogs include rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and adenovirus.
Core vaccines for cats include rabies, feline herpesvirus, calicivirus, and
panleukopenia. Schedule depends on age and local regulations — consult a vet.

## Parasite prevention
Year-round flea, tick, and heartworm prevention is recommended in most regions.
Talk to your vet about the right product for your pet's species, weight, and
local risk profile.

## When to see a vet immediately
Seek emergency care for: difficulty breathing, repeated vomiting, bloody
stool, suspected toxin ingestion, sudden collapse, or seizures. This guide
is not a substitute for professional veterinary advice.
