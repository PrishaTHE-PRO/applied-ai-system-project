# Grooming

## Brushing
Short-haired dogs and cats: weekly brushing is usually enough. Long-haired
breeds may need daily brushing to prevent mats.

## Bathing
Most dogs do well with a bath every 4 to 8 weeks unless they get especially
dirty. Cats generally groom themselves and rarely need baths.

## Nails, ears, teeth
Trim nails every 3 to 4 weeks. Check ears weekly for redness, odor, or
discharge. Brush teeth several times per week with pet-safe toothpaste —
human toothpaste is toxic to pets.
